# Three Bit Counter
A [[Electronics/Counters/Asynchronous/3 Bit/Three Bit Counter]] can count up to three. It's made by connecting three [[Electronics/Counters/Asynchronous/1 Bit/One Bit Counter|One Bit Counters]] in series.
