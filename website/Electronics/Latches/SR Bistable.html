SR Bistable

An SR Bistable latch has two stable states (bi - two). As they are stable states the latch will stay in one of it's stable states until it is forced into it's other stable state.

The bar above the letters means that they are Active Low

Inside a SR Bistable is a circuit made up of NAND gates, as shown bellow.

Truth Table for a SR Bistable:

A SR Bistable isn't commonly represented by a Truth Table, since the output of the Integrated Circuit depends on the previous output.

Instead these types of Circuits are represented using a Sequence Table:

Or a Sequence Diagram

Problems

However, they are some problems with the SR Bistable using Active Low inputs.

  • and cannot be low at the same time.
  • Active Low is often hard to think about.
  • The output changes as soon as or changes.
